COVID19 Dataset: (StimulusPackG20shareGDP)
COVID19 Value of COVID-19 fiscal stimulus packages in G20 countries as a % share of GDP

 
File naming convention:
   <dataset_name>-<YYYYMMDD>.CSV

Field description:
Country				: G20 Country
StimulusPercentShareOfGDP	: Value of stimulus packages in % share of GDP 2020


Update frequency:
Monthly

Data sources:

https://www.statista.com/statistics/1107572/covid-19-value-g20-stimulus-packages-share-gdp/

Source	IMF
Conducted by	IMF
Survey period	April 2020
Note	*The total cost of the Russian fiscal stimulus package is estimated to cost between one and 1.5 percent of the country's GDP.
Original source	imf.org
