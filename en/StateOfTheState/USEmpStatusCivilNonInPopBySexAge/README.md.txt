COVID19 Dataset: (USEmpStatusCivilNonInPopBySexAge)
HOUSEHOLD DATA US Total Employment status of the civilian noninstitutional population by sex and age, seasonally adjusted[Numbers in thousands]
 
File naming convention:
   <dataset_name>-<YYYYMMDD>.CSV

Field description:
MonthYear				: Month & Year
CivilNoninstPopu			: Total Civilian noninstitutional population	
CivilLaborForce				: Total  Civilian labor force
ParticipationRate			: Participation rate
Employed				: Total Employed	
EmploymentPopuRatio			: Employment-population ratio
Unemployed				: Total Unemployed	
UnemploymentRate			: Unemployment rate
NotInlaborForce				: Total Not in labor force
PersonsCurrentlyWantAjob		: Total Persons who currently want a job
	
	
NOTE: The population figures are not adjusted for seasonal variation.Updated population controls are introduced annually with the release of January data.

Update frequency:
Monthly

API details : https://www.bls.gov/developers/home.htm

Data sources:
The US Bureau of Labor Statistics' (BLS)
